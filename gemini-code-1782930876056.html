  • How have learning methods changed over time? (Cách 4)
💡 HOW TO ANSWER: Part 1: Trả lời trực tiếp = paraphrasing the question | Part 2: Expand the questions
1. Do you think doctors should be paid more? Why?
Cách 1: List ideas (2 ideas)
Outline
high-pressure job / hold lives in hands / many years of intense training
Sample Answer
"Personally, I definitely think medical professionals deserve higher salaries. Firstly, their job is extremely high-pressure, as they hold people's lives in their hands every day. Secondly, they have to spend many years undergoing intense training, so a high income is a fair reward."
2. Do you think doctors should be paid more? Why?
Cách 2: Depends on (Experience & Specialization)
Outline
depends on years of experience / senior doctors vs graduates / specialization / surgeons vs general practitioners
Sample Answer
"I think this disparity depends on experience and specialization. Senior doctors with decades of experience naturally earn more than fresh graduates. Furthermore, specialized fields like brain surgery require higher expertise, so their pay is much higher than general practitioners."
3. Why should children learn biology?
Cách 3: Deep dive (1 idea)
Outline
satisfy natural curiosity / understand the world / explore plants and animals / keep them hooked
Sample Answer
"Yes, I absolutely think so. This is mainly because biology satisfies children's natural curiosity about the world. For example, when kids learn how plants grow or how animals behave, they feel connected to nature, which keeps them hooked on the subject."
4. How have learning methods changed over time?
Cách 4: Contexts (Methods)
Outline
past: passive learning, memorizing / now: interactive learning, trial and error
Sample Answer
"Learning methods have changed significantly. In the past, people learned passively by memorizing facts from textbooks. Currently, people prefer interactive learning and trial-and-error, which helps them understand things deeply through practical experience."
